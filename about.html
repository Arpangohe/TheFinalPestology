Our Story

Our Mission & Vision

We are a multifaceted solution company formed to provide ease of living in 2022. From Pest Management to Hygiene Solutions, we intend to provide various hassle-free Facility solutions for day-to-day problems with our seamless services through the best quality products.

Based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, The Pestology has quickly established itself as a trusted name in facility solutions. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and environmental responsibility has helped us build strong relationships with clients across various sectors.

We believe in providing comprehensive solutions that address the unique needs of each client, ensuring their spaces remain safe, clean, and well-maintained.

Sectors We Serve

Providing comprehensive solutions across various industries

Schools & University Campus

We provide comprehensive facility solutions for educational institutions, ensuring clean, safe, and pest-free environments for students and staff. Our services include regular pest control, sanitization, and maintenance to meet the specific needs of schools and universities.

Construction Sites

Construction sites face unique challenges when it comes to pest control and waterproofing. We offer specialized solutions to protect construction materials, prevent pest infestations, and ensure proper waterproofing during the construction process.

Residential Homes

We help homeowners maintain pest-free, clean, and well-maintained living spaces. Our residential services include pest control, sanitization, waterproofing, and other solutions tailored to the specific needs of each home.

Commercial Offices

We provide comprehensive facility solutions for commercial offices, ensuring clean, professional, and pest-free work environments. Our services help businesses maintain their premises and create a positive impression on clients and employees.

Restaurants

Restaurants require stringent pest control and hygiene measures. We offer specialized solutions to help food establishments maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and comply with health regulations.

Factories

We provide industrial-strength facility solutions for factories, addressing the unique challenges of manufacturing environments. Our services help maintain clean, safe, and efficient production facilities.
