General Pest Control: Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Keep your home clean and free of food debris
  • Store food in sealed containers
  • Regularly empty trash and keep bins clean
  • Fix leaky pipes and eliminate standing water
  • Seal cracks and gaps in walls, floors, and foundations
  • Keep vegetation trimmed away from your home
  • Regularly inspect your home for signs of pest activity
  • Schedule regular professional pest control treatments
  • Vacuum regularly, especially in hard-to-reach areas
  • Follow all post-treatment instructions from your pest control provider

Don'ts

  • Don't leave dirty dishes in the sink overnight
  • Don't allow clutter to accumulate, as it provides hiding places for pests
  • Don't leave pet food out overnight
  • Don't ignore small pest problems, as they can quickly grow
  • Don't use DIY pest control products without proper knowledge
  • Don't mix different pest control products
  • Don't enter treated areas before it's safe to do so
  • Don't wash treated surfaces immediately after application
  • Don't allow children or pets near pest control products
  • Don't forget to address the underlying causes of pest problems
